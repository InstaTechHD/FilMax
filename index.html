Description

Filmax is a cross-platform mobile app where you can find movies, series, seasons, episodes, movie recommendation and actors from the largest movie database. With this app, you can also find season and movie ratings to make a solid decision on what to watch next.

With FilMax, you have movies and series information right at your fingertips! With a lot of features:

View movies/series details such as ratings, cast, seasons, similars and other details Search for movies, series and actors Get personal movie suggestions based on your interest
